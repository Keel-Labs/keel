Keel is your personal AI chief of staff — an intelligent assistant that knows your projects, priorities, and preferences.

Unlike generic chatbots, Keel maintains a persistent "brain" of your work context: active projects, key people, daily logs, and tasks. Every conversation is informed by what matters to you.

KEY FEATURES

• Smart Context — Keel reads your projects, notes, and daily logs to give relevant, personalized answers
• Multiple AI Models — Choose between Claude, GPT-4o, OpenRouter, or run local models with Ollama
• Daily Briefs — Automated morning summaries of your priorities and schedule
• Memory — Keel remembers what you tell it across conversations
• Reminders — Set natural language reminders that notify you at the right time
• Knowledge Browser — View and edit your brain files directly
• Privacy First — Your data stays yours. Self-host the server or use our cloud
• Cross-Platform — Syncs with the Keel desktop app on Mac and Windows

GETTING STARTED

1. Create a free Keel cloud account (or connect to your self-hosted server)
2. Add an API key for your preferred AI provider
3. Start chatting — Keel learns your context over time

If you already use Keel on desktop, use "Sync to Cloud" in desktop Settings to migrate your data.
