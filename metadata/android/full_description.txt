Keel is your personal AI chief of staff — an intelligent assistant that knows your projects, priorities, and preferences.

Unlike generic chatbots, Keel maintains a persistent "brain" of your work context: active projects, key people, daily logs, and tasks. Every conversation is informed by what matters to you.

<b>Key Features</b>

• <b>Smart Context</b> — Keel reads your projects, notes, and daily logs to give relevant, personalized answers
• <b>Multiple AI Models</b> — Choose between Claude, GPT-4o, OpenRouter, or run local models with Ollama
• <b>Daily Briefs</b> — Automated morning summaries of your priorities and schedule
• <b>Memory</b> — Keel remembers what you tell it across conversations
• <b>Reminders</b> — Set natural language reminders that notify you at the right time
• <b>Knowledge Browser</b> — View and edit your brain files directly
• <b>Privacy First</b> — Your data stays yours. Self-host the server or use our cloud
• <b>Cross-Platform</b> — Syncs with the Keel desktop app on Mac and Windows

<b>Getting Started</b>

1. Create a free Keel cloud account (or connect to your self-hosted server)
2. Add an API key for your preferred AI provider
3. Start chatting — Keel learns your context over time

If you already use Keel on desktop, use "Sync to Cloud" in desktop Settings to migrate your data.
