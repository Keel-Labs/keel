# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone who participates in the Keel community, in any capacity, will experience a harassment-free environment regardless of:

- Age
- Body size
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience
- Nationality
- Personal appearance
- Race
- Religion
- Sexual identity and orientation

This Code of Conduct applies to all Keel community spaces, including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any official events.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that create a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Asking questions before making assumptions
- Giving credit to others for their ideas and contributions
- Helping others learn and grow

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation
- Discriminatory jokes or language
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal attacks or name-calling
- Publishing others' private information ("doxxing")
- Deliberate misinformation or bad-faith arguments
- Spam or off-topic content
- Any form of violence or threats of violence

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by emailing **medhaforcebewithyou@gmail.com** with:

- A clear description of what happened
- When it occurred
- Any relevant links or context
- Whether you'd like to remain anonymous

All reports will be taken seriously and reviewed promptly.

### Consequences

Community leaders will:
- Investigate reports fairly and objectively
- Maintain confidentiality of reporters
- Take appropriate action, which may include:
  - A private warning or conversation
  - Temporary restrictions on participation
  - Permanent removal from the community

The goal is to maintain a safe, respectful community while giving people the opportunity to learn and improve.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All Keel community spaces (GitHub, discussions, issues, etc.)
- Official community events and meetups
- Communications made on behalf of Keel

Personal conduct outside these spaces is not subject to this Code of Conduct, unless it directly impacts the safety or reputation of the Keel community.
